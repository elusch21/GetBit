CoinConnect Documentation

Overview

Cryptocurrency is a digital currency that uses encryption techniques to generate and transfer funds, separate from a bank.
CoinConnect uses data from a free API to bring you up to date information on over 10,000 different cryptocurrency coins.
Use this application to gain valuable trader information or just for general curiosity.

CoinConnect With Account (Reccomended)

By signing up for a free account with CoinConnect, you gain access to a personalized list of your favorite coins.
You may add or delete coins from this list, but whenever you log back in, your list will be the same as when you left it.
Simply click the update button to get up to date information on up to 8 of your coins!

CoinConnect Without Account

From the homepage of CoinConenct, you may retrieve information about any of the top six ranked cryptocurrency coins.
The top six coins at any time will be updated constantly.
Some of the information available includes record price, current price, and the price 24 hours from your request.

Security

At CoinConnect, we value your security as much as you do.
For that reason, we make sure to keep all of your account information protected.
We use encryption techniques to hide and store your information in our database.
