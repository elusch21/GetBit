GetBit Documentation

Overview

GetBit uses data from a free API to bring you up to date information on over 10,000 different cryptocurrency coins.
Use this application to gain valuable trader information or just for general curiosity.

How to Use GetBit (No Account)

From the homepage of GetBit, simply enter the 3-4 digit symbol (ticker) representing the coin
and press the button to receive the relavant information.
Without an account, you may enter one coin at a time and get that coin's most recent information.

How to Use GetBit (With Account)

By signing up for a free account with GetBit, you gain access to a personalized list of your favorite coins.
You may add or delete coins from this list, but whenever you log back in, your list will be the same as when you left it.
Simply click the update button to get up to date information on all of your coins!
